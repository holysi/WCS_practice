🗣️ Full Dialogue: Meeting a Cosplayer at Oasis 21

Leo: Excuse me, hi! I am so sorry to bother you while you are resting, but I just had to come over and say that your cosplay looks absolutely amazing! The attention to detail on your armor is incredible.
（不好意思，嗨！真的很抱歉在妳休息的時候打擾妳，但我一定要走過來跟妳說，妳的 Cosplay 看起來真的太棒了！妳盔甲上的細節處理簡直不可思議。）
Sora: Oh, wow! Thank you so much! You didn't bother me at all, I was just checking my schedule for the afternoon. I really appreciate your kind words, especially about the armor. It took me three whole months to finish making it!
（噢，哇！非常謝謝你！你完全沒有打擾到我，我剛剛只是在確認下午的行程。真的很感謝你的讚美，特別是關於盔甲的部分。這整整花了我三個月的時間才製作完成！）
Leo: Three months? That is serious dedication! It definitely paid off because the paint job and the weathering look so realistic. If you have some time right now, would it be alright if I took a few photos of you? I have my DSLR camera ready.
（三個月？這真的是極大的專注與奉獻！這絕對是值得的，因為漆面和舊化效果看起來太逼真了。如果妳現在有時間的話，可以讓我幫妳拍幾張照片嗎？我的單眼相機已經準備好了。）
Sora: Yes, absolutely! I would love to. Just give me a quick second to adjust my wig and grab my prop sword. Alright, I am ready! Let me just get into character and strike a few poses for you. How is the lighting over here?
（好啊，當然可以！我很樂意。請給我一秒鐘調整一下假髮並拿我的道具劍。好的，我準備好了！讓我進入角色狀態並幫你擺幾個姿勢。這裡的光線還可以嗎？）
Leo: The lighting here under the Oasis 21 roof is perfect. It gives a nice, soft even glow. Alright, hold that pose right there... perfect! Look slightly over my left shoulder... great! One more, smile a little bit... fantastic! Wow, these are turning out beautifully.
（綠洲 21 屋頂下的光線非常完美。它帶來了很好、很柔和且均勻的光芒。好的，保持那個姿勢不要動... 完美！稍微看著我的左肩膀上方... 太棒了！再一張，稍微微笑一下... 太讚了！哇，這些照片拍出來的效果非常漂亮。）
Sora: Awesome! Can I take a quick look at the camera screen? Oh, my goodness! These look incredible! You are a very talented photographer. The angles you chose make my costume look even more dynamic. Thank you so much.
（太棒了！我可以快速看一下相機螢幕嗎？噢，我的天啊！這些看起來太不可思議了！你是一位非常有才華的攝影師。你選擇的角度讓我的服裝看起來更有動態感。非常謝謝你。）
Leo: Thank you! It is easy when the model is so professional. By the way, which character are you cosplaying? I know the series is a famous sports anime, but I want to make sure I get the character's name exactly right.
（謝謝妳！當模特兒這麼專業時，拍照就變得很容易。順帶一提，妳 Cos 的是哪個角色？我知道這個作品是一部很有名的運動動漫，但我希望能確保把角色的名字記到完全正確。）
Sora: I am cosplaying the captain of the team! He has a very strong and reliable personality, which is why I love him so much. Is this your first time attending the World Cosplay Summit here in Nagoya?
（我 Cos 的是隊長！他有一個非常強大且可靠的個性，這也是為什麼我這麼喜歡他。這是你第一次來名古屋參加世界 Cosplay 高峰會嗎？）
Leo: Yes, it is! I flew in just two days ago, and the atmosphere here is absolutely electric. I've never seen so many amazing cosplayers in one place. Since you mentioned you were checking your schedule, are you participating in the parade later?
（是的，沒錯！我兩天前才飛過來，這裡的氣氛簡直熱血沸騰。我從來沒有在同一個地方看過這麼多厲害的 Cosplayer。既然妳剛才提到在確認行程，妳晚點會參加遊行嗎？）

（會的，我要參加！遊行今天下午晚些時候會在大須觀音開始。天氣會非常熱，但這總是活動中最令人興奮的部分。噢，在忘記之前，你有 Instagram 或 X 的帳號嗎？我們應該互換一下社群媒體！）
Leo: Definitely! I would love to tag you when I finish editing and uploading these photos. Here is my QR code on my phone. My handle is @Leo_Photo_WCS. Feel free to scan it!
（一定要的！我很樂意在修完圖並上傳這些照片時標記妳。這是我手機上的 QR code。我的帳號是 @Leo_Photo_WCS。請隨意掃描！）
Sora: Perfect, I just started following you! My handle is @Sora_Cos. I can't wait to see the final photos. By the way, since you are new to Nagoya, have you had lunch yet? You look a bit tired from carrying that heavy camera equipment around.
（太好了，我剛剛追蹤你了！我的帳號是 @Sora_Cos。我等不及想看到最終的照片了。順帶一提，既然你第一次來名古屋，你吃過午餐了嗎？你提著那麼重的相機設備走來走去，看起來有點累了。）
Leo: Actually, I haven't eaten anything since breakfast. I was too excited looking at all the costumes! Do you have any good recommendations for local food around here that isn't too far away?
（事實上，我自從早餐後就什麼都沒吃了。我光看所有的服裝就太興奮了！妳這附近有推薦什麼不會太遠的在地美食嗎？）
Sora: You are in luck! Right downstairs inside Oasis 21, or over at the nearby Sakae district, there are great restaurants. You absolutely must try Nagoya's famous *Tebasaki*, which are crispy fried chicken wings with a sweet and spicy glaze. They are perfect for regaining your energy!
（你運氣很好！就在綠洲 21 的樓下，或者在附近的榮（Sakae）商圈就有很棒的餐廳。你絕對必須嚐嚐名古屋著名的「手羽先」，那是炸得酥脆並裹上鮮甜微辣醬汁的雞翅。這用來恢復體力再完美不過了！）
Leo: That sounds mouth-watering and exactly what I need right now. Thank you so much for the recommendation, and thank you again for letting me take your photos. Good luck with the parade this afternoon!
（這聽起來令人垂涎三尺，而且完全就是我現在需要的。非常謝謝妳的推薦，也再次謝謝妳讓我拍照。祝妳今天下午的遊行一切順利！）
Sora: Thank you! It was great meeting you, Leo. Enjoy the rest of the World Cosplay Summit, and have a wonderful time in Nagoya! Take care!
（謝謝你！很高興認識你，Leo。享受接下來的世界 Cosplay 高峰會，並在名古屋度過美好的時光！保重囉！）
