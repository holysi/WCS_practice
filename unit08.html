I love how reliable, hardworking, and silently passionate he is. He isn't the flashiest player on the court, but he holds the whole team together like a solid anchor.
（噢，非常謝謝你！能從攝影師口中聽到這句話對我來說意義重大。是的，他絕對 corner 我的「推（Oshi）」——我的一號最愛！我喜歡他是多麼地可靠、努力，且默默地充滿熱情。他不是球場上最耀眼的球員，但他像一個穩固的錨一樣把整個球隊凝聚在一起。）
Leo: I totally get it. That sports anime is an absolute masterpiece. The character development is so profound. My favorite dynamic is actually the intense rivalry and ultimate trust between the genius setter and the energetic middle blocker. Every time they pull off that super-fast attack, it gets my adrenaline pumping!
（我完全懂。那部運動動漫絕對是神作。角色發展非常深刻。我最喜歡的動態其實是那位天才舉球員與充滿活力的副攻手之間，那種激烈的競爭意識與終極的信任。每次他們成功打出超快攻時，都會讓我腎上腺素飆升！）
Sora: Right?! That combo is legendary! The way the story shows that "you can jump because I am here to back you up" is so emotional. It makes me cry every single time I rewatch the tournament arc. Which match was the most memorable one for you?
（對吧？！那個組合簡直是傳奇！故事展現出「因為有我在背後撐著，你才能儘管起跳」的方式太令人動容了。每次我重溫全國大賽篇時，真的都會看一次哭一次。對你來說哪一場比賽最令你難忘？）
Leo: Hands down, the match against the powerhouse school from Miyagi. The rallies were so long and intense. The animation quality during the final point, where the sound completely drops out and everything moves in slow motion... man, that was pure cinematic art.
（毫無疑問，是跟宮城縣那所強豪學校的對決。來回對攻拉鋸得又長又激烈。在最後一分時的動畫品質，聲音完全消失、一切都變成慢動作處理... 天哪，那純粹是電影藝術。）

（噢，我完全知道你說的是哪個畫面！那讓人屏息。順帶一提，你也看黑暗奇幻類的動漫嗎？我也非常沉迷於那些主角強度爆表（傲天）的系列，像是奇幻國度裡的魔王或至高無上的統治者。）
Leo: Are you talking about series like Overlord? Because if you are, I am a massive fan! I absolutely love the dark, strategic world-building, and the loyalty of the floor guardians is just incredible. The contrast between the protagonist's inner monologue as a regular salaryman and his majestic outer appearance is hilarious yet epic.
（妳是在說像《Overlord》這樣的系列嗎？如果是的話，我是個超級大粉絲！我絕對超愛那種黑暗、充滿策略的世界觀建立，而且階層守護者們的忠誠度簡直不可思議。主角內心作為一個普通上班族的內心獨白，與他外在莊嚴威嚴的形象之間的對比，既搞笑又史詩感十足。）
Sora: Yes! Exactly! I actually planned to cosplay the pure-white devil overseer character next year, with the massive black wings. But the engineering required to make those wings look majestic and remain lightweight is a huge challenge.
（沒錯！就是那個！我其實計畫明年要 Cosplay 那位擁有巨大黑色羽翼的純白惡魔總管角色。但是，要讓那些翅膀看起來神聖莊嚴又維持輕量化，所需的工程結構是一個巨大的挑戰。）
Leo: Oh, please do it! If you cosplay her, let me know immediately. I can already picture the ideal lighting setup. We could use heavy contrast, moody fluorescent lighting, and perhaps some fog effects to create that authentic, dark cinematic aesthetic.
（噢，請一定要出！如果妳 Cos 她的話，請立刻通知我。我腦海中已經浮現出理想的光影佈局了。我們可以用強烈的高對比、情緒化的螢光調性，甚至加一些煙霧特效，來營造那種正宗、黑暗的電影美學。）
Sora: Wow, high-contrast fluorescent lighting? That sounds perfect for her vibe! It's so refreshing to meet a photographer who understands both sports anime passion and dark fantasy aesthetics. Talking with you makes me want to start working on my next costume right away!
（哇，高對比螢光？這聽起來對她的氣場來說太完美了！能遇到一位既懂運動動漫的熱血、又懂黑暗奇幻美學的攝影師，真的太讓人振奮了。跟你聊天讓我現在就想立刻動工做我的下一套服裝！）
Leo: Haha, that's the power of the World Cosplay Summit! It brings passionate creators together. Let's stay in touch on X, and whenever you start working on those wings, you can share the progress. I'd love to see how you engineer it.
（哈哈，這就是世界 Cosplay 高峰會的力量！它把充滿熱情的創作者聚集在一起。我們在 X 上保持聯絡，不論妳什麼時候開始著手做那對翅膀，都可以分享進度。我很想看看妳是怎麼把它結構設計出來的。）
Sora: Deal! I'll make sure to post my progress logs. Thanks for the inspiring chat, Leo. It's the perfect ending to an amazing convention day!
（一言為定！我一定會發布我的製作日誌。謝謝這場帶給我靈感的對話，Leo。這為超讚的展場一天劃下了完美的句點！）
