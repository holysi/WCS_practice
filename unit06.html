Unit 06: Queuing at the Popular Booth!

體驗 WCS 商品販售區、大手攤位購買周邊與同人誌的排隊對話（3-5分鐘精實特輯）

🔊 課文朗讀 (TTS 慢速學習)：

🗣️ Full Dialogue: Navigating the Merchandise Hall at WCS

Leo: Wow, look at this massive crowd! The exhibition hall for merchandise is absolutely packed today. Sora, is that insanely long line over there the queue for the official World Cosplay Summit goods?
（哇，看看這龐大的人潮！今天商品展覽館內簡直擠爆了。Sora，那邊排得誇張長的隊伍，是購買 WCS 官方周邊的隊列嗎？）
Sora: No, actually that line is for a famous "wall circle" booth! A legendary illustrator is selling limited-edition artbooks and exclusive merchandise there today. In anime conventions, we call these extremely popular booths "major booths" or "wall booths" because they have to be placed near the walls for crowd control.
（不，其實那個隊伍是一家很有名的「牆壁攤（大手攤位）」！今天有一位傳奇插畫家在那裡販售限量版畫冊和獨家周邊。在動漫展裡，我們把這種超級熱門的攤位叫做「大手攤」或「牆壁攤」，因為為了控制人潮，他們必須被安排在靠近牆壁的位置。）
Leo: Ah, I see! That makes perfect sense. I am actually looking for the booth that sells the exclusive collaboration badges and acrylic stands for that sports anime series. Do you happen to know where it is located?
（啊，我懂了！這完全說得通。我其實正在尋找販售那部運動動漫系列聯名限定徽章和壓克力立牌的攤位。妳碰巧知道它在哪個位置嗎？）
Sora: Yes! According to the floor map, it's located at Booth B-12, right around the corner. Oh wait, look over there. Someone is holding up a sign that says "End of the Line." That means the queue starts all the way back here! We should go find the end of the line immediately before it gets any longer.
（有的！根據場地平面圖，它位於 B-12 攤位，就在那個轉角處。喔等等，看那邊。有人正舉著一個寫有「隊尾（最後尾）」的牌子。這代表隊伍一路延伸到這裡來了！我們應該立刻去那裡排隊，免得隊伍變得更長。）
Leo: *[Walking to the queue]* Excuse me, is this the back of the line for the anime collaboration booth?
（*［走向隊伍］* 不好意思，請問這是動漫聯名攤位的隊尾嗎？）
Attendee: Yes, it is! I am currently the last person. Here, please take this sign and hold it up so the next person knows where to line up.
（是的，沒錯！我目前是最後一個人。來，請接下這塊牌子並把它舉好，好讓下一個人知道要在哪裡排隊。）
Leo: Oh, thank you! So, this is how it works. Sora, since I have to hold this sign for a bit, can you check the menu on your phone? I'm worried the items might sell out before we reach the front.
（噢，謝謝你！原來運作機制是這樣。Sora，既然我得拿著這個牌子一陣子，妳能用手機確認一下商品明細嗎？我很擔心在我們排到前面之前，商品就已經完售了。）
Sora: Let me check their official X account updates... Oh, good news! They just posted that everything is still in stock. However, there is a strict purchase limit. Each person can only buy a maximum of two items per merchandise category.
（讓我查一下他們官方 X 的即時更新... 喔，好消息！他們剛發文說目前所有商品都還有庫存。不過，有限制嚴格的限購數量。每人每個商品類別最多只能購買兩件。）
Leo: A purchase limit is actually a relief. It prevents scalpers from buying up everything instantly. Hey, look, the line is moving pretty fast! We are already halfway there.
（有限購數量其實讓人鬆了一口氣。這能防止黃牛瞬間把所有東西一掃而空。嘿，看，隊伍前進得挺快的！我們已經走完一半了。）
Sora: *[Reaching the front of the booth]* Alright, we are next! Do you want to buy the artbook as well? If you buy the complete set, it comes with an exclusive bonus postcard and a special shopping bag!
（*［排到攤位前］* 好的，下一個就輪到我們了！你也要連同畫冊一起買嗎？如果你購買全套的話，還會附贈一張獨家特典明信片和一個特別的購物袋喔！）
Leo: Definitely! I'll take the full set. *[To the booth staff]* Hello, I'd like one complete set, please. Also, can I add two random acrylic keychains?
（一定要的！我要買全套。*［對攤位工作人員］* 你好，麻煩給我一套全套商品。另外，我可以再加購兩個隨機壓克力鑰匙圈嗎？）
Staff: Certainly! That will be one complete set and two blind-box keychains. Your total comes to 6,500 yen. We only accept cash or PayPay today. Will that be alright?
（好的！一套全套商品和兩個盲盒鑰匙圈。總共是 6500 日圓。我們今天只接受現金或 PayPay。這樣可以嗎？）
Leo: Cash is perfect, here you go. Thank you so much! *[Receiving the goods]* Yes! I finally got it! Look at the print quality on this bag, Sora. It's beautiful!
（現金沒問題，給你。非常謝謝你！*［接過商品］* 耶！我終於買到了！看這個袋子上的印刷品質，Sora。太漂亮了！）
Sora: Awesome! I'm so glad you got the full set before anything sold out. Now that our hands are full of merchandise, let's find a spot to check our blind boxes and see which characters you drew!
（太棒了！我真高興妳在任何東西完售前買到了全套。既然我們現在雙手拿滿了戰利品（周邊），我們去找個地方開盲盒，看看你抽中哪些角色吧！）
