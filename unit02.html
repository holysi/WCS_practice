Unit 02: Strike a Cool Pose!

指導拍照動作、調整光線與攝影角度互動（3-5分鐘精實特輯）

🔊 課文朗讀 (TTS 慢速學習)：

🗣️ Full Dialogue: Directing and Posing at the Venue

Leo: Alright, Sora! Let's find a slightly better spot for the photoshoot. The sun is directly overhead right now, which creates harsh shadows under your eyes. Could we move over to that shaded area next to the brick wall?
（好的，Sora！我們來找一個稍微好一點的拍攝地點。現在太陽正懸在頭頂上，會在妳的眼睛下方造成很重的陰影。我們可以移到磚牆旁邊的那個陰影區嗎？）
Sora: Ah, that makes sense! Harsh lighting is a nightmare for cosplay photos. How is this spot? The light feels much softer here under the shadow of the building.
（啊，有道理！強烈的直射光對 Cosplay 照片來說簡直是噩夢。這個位置怎麼樣？建築物陰影下的光線感覺柔和多了。）
Leo: This is perfect. The background is nice and clean too. First, let's start with a few full-body shots to capture your whole costume. Could you step forward a bit? Perfect. Now, please turn your body at a three-quarter angle toward the camera.
（這裡完美。背景也很乾淨漂亮。首先，我們從幾張全身照開始，來捕捉妳整套服裝。可以稍微往前走一步嗎？完美。現在，請把身體轉向面對相機三分之二的角度。）
Sora: Like this? Should I look directly into the lens, or should I look away to create a more dramatic, cinematic mood?
（像這樣嗎？我應該直接直視鏡頭，還是把視線移開，好營造出一種更具戲劇感、電影感的氛圍呢？）
Leo: For the first few shots, looking away would be amazing. Try looking slightly over my left shoulder, as if you are gazing at a distant enemy. Yes! That's it! Now, raise your prop sword slightly and point it forward.
（前幾張照片的話，把視線移開會非常棒。試著稍微看著我左肩的上方，就像妳正注視著遠處的敵人一樣。對！就是這樣！現在，稍微舉起妳的道具劍並指向前方。）
Sora: Got it. Let me adjust my grip on the sword hilt. Is this height okay? I don't want the blade to block my face or cast a shadow on my expressions.
（收到。讓我調整一下握劍柄的手勢。這個高度可以嗎？我不希望劍刃擋住我的臉，或在我的表情上投下陰影。）
Leo: That height is perfect. Hold that pose right there. I'm going to crouch down to get a low-angle shot. This will make you look much more heroic and powerful, like a true captain. One, two, three... click! Beautiful!
（那個高度完美。保持那個姿勢不要動。我要蹲下來拍一張低角度的照片。這樣會讓妳看起來更具英雄氣概且強大，就像一個真正的隊長。一、二、三... 喀擦！漂亮！）
Sora: Awesome! Let's do some close-up shots next. I want to make sure we capture the makeup details and the contact lenses clearly. Should I change my expression to a more serious one?
（太棒了！接下來我們拍一些特寫鏡頭吧。我想確保能清晰拍下化妝的細節跟角膜變色片。我應該把表情換成更嚴肅的嗎？）
Leo: Yes, please! Give me a sharp, fierce glare. I will zoom in a bit for a tight headshot. Wait, your wig has a few stray hairs across your forehead. Could you tuck them behind your ear or smooth them down?
（是的，麻煩了！給我一個銳利、兇悍的眼神。我會稍微放大鏡頭來拍一張緊湊的頭部特寫。等等，妳的假髮額前有幾根雜髮。可以把它們塞到耳後，或是撫平一下嗎？）
Sora: Oh, thanks for noticing! Wig maintenance during an outdoor summer con is so difficult. Is it better now?
（噢，謝謝你注意到！夏天在室外場次維持假髮真的太困難了。現在有好一點了嗎？）
Leo: Much better! Perfect. Okay, fierce glare... look straight into the lens this time. Excellent! The reflection of the light in your contact lenses looks incredible. It really brings the character to life.
（好多了！完美。好的，兇悍的眼神... 這次請直視鏡頭。太有張力了！光線在妳變色片裡的倒影看起來不可思議。真的把角色演活了。）
Sora: Thank you! I practiced this expression in front of the mirror so many times. Can we try an action pose next? Like an over-the-shoulder look while pretending to run away or look back in surprise?
（謝謝你！我在鏡子前練習這個表情好多次了。接下來我們可以試試看動作特寫嗎？像是假裝往前跑一邊回頭看，或者驚訝回眸的姿勢？）
Leo: I love that idea! It adds so much movement. For an over-the-shoulder shot, turn your back completely to me first. Then, twist your upper body and head back toward the camera on my count. I will use a fast shutter speed to capture the motion crisp and clear. Ready? Three, two, one, look back!
（我超愛這個點子！這增添了好多躍動感。回眸特寫的話，請先完全背對我。然後在我倒數時，把上半身和頭扭轉回來看著相機。我會用高速快門來清晰捕捉這個動態。準備好了嗎？三、二、一，回頭！）
Sora: *Twists back and dynamic pose* Did you catch it? It felt really smooth on my end!
（*轉身並擺出動態姿勢* 你有抓拍到嗎？我這邊感覺做得很順暢！）
Leo: Yes, I got it! The composition is incredible, and your cape flared out perfectly behind you. This is definitely going to be one of the best shots of the day!
（有，我拍到了！構圖太不可思議了，而且妳的披風在妳身後完美地飄揚開來。這絕對會成為今天拍得最好的照片之一！）

📝 Key Vocabulary

Word / Phrase 中文解釋 例句
Harsh shadows 強烈/死黑的陰影 Harsh shadows can ruin a good portrait photo.
Three-quarter angle 三分之二斜側面角度 Turn at a three-quarter angle for a slimmer look.
Low-angle shot 低角度仰拍 A low-angle shot makes the subject look heroic.
Close-up shot 特寫鏡頭 We need a close-up shot of the intricate makeup.
