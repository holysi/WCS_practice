Unit 07: Emergency! My Prop is Broken

現場服裝或道具損壞時，向他人借用緊急維修工具的求助對話（3-5分鐘精實特輯）

🔊 課文朗讀 (TTS 慢速學習)：

🗣️ Full Dialogue: Dealing with a Cosplay Malfunction

Sora: Oh no! Oh my goodness, Leo, we have a major crisis! The strap on my right shoulder armor just snapped completely off! I think someone accidentally bumped into me in the crowded merchandise hall.
（糟糕！天啊，Leo，我們遇到大危機了！我右肩盔甲上的帶子整個斷掉了！我想剛剛在擁擠的商品館裡有人不小心撞到我了。）
Leo: Oh shoot, that's terrible! Let me take a look at it. It seems the hot glue joint connecting the strap to the EVA foam broke apart. Don't panic! Do you have a cosplay emergency repair kit in your bag?
（啊，糟糕，這太慘了！讓我看一下。看起來是連接帶子和 EVA 泡棉的熱熔膠接合處斷開了。別慌！妳包包裡有帶 Cosplay 緊急維修包嗎？）
Sora: I usually bring one, but I forgot to pack it this morning because I was rushing to get to the venue on time. I am so mad at myself! The parade is going to start in an hour, and I can't walk around with my shoulder armor dangling like this.
（我平時都會帶，但今天早上因為趕著準時到會場就忘了放進包包。我真的氣死我自己了！遊行一小時後就要開始了，我不能讓肩甲像這樣晃來晃去地到處走。）
Leo: It's okay, mistakes happen. Let's look around the dressing area. Maybe we can ask another cosplayer or photographer for help. Look, there's a group of cosplayers sitting over there with a lot of luggage. Let's go ask them. Excuse me, hi! I am so sorry to bother you.
（沒關係，人都會犯錯。我們在更衣區附近找找看。也許我們可以向其他 Cosplayer 或攝影師尋求幫助。看，那邊有一群帶了很多行李的 Cosplayer 坐在那。我們去問問看。不好意思，嗨！真的很抱歉打擾你們。）
Cosplayer B: Hi there! No problem at all. Is everything okay? You two look a bit worried.
（你們好！完全沒關係。還順利嗎？你們兩個看起來有點焦慮。）
Leo: We are having a bit of a cosplay malfunction. The shoulder strap on her armor just broke. By any chance, do you happen to have any safety pins, super glue, or perhaps a portable hot glue gun that we could borrow for a few minutes?
（我們的服裝道具出了點故障。她盔甲上的肩帶剛好斷了。不曉得你們手邊碰巧有沒有安全別針、瞬間膠，或者行動熱熔膠槍，可以借我們用個幾分鐘嗎？）
Cosplayer B: Oh, I've been there! Prop damage is the worst. Let me check my emergency kit... I don't have a portable glue gun, but I do have some heavy-duty double-sided tape, a bottle of instant super glue, and plenty of safety pins. Will any of these work?
（噢，我完全懂這種心情！道具損壞最讓人崩潰了。讓我查一下我的救急包... 我沒有行動熱熔膠槍，但我有一些高強度的雙面膠、一瓶瞬間膠和很多安全別針。這些有哪樣能派上用場嗎？）
Sora: The instant super glue would be an absolute lifesaver for the foam! Also, could I borrow two safety pins just in case the glue doesn't hold under the heat? I want to pin the strap from the inside as a backup.
（這瞬間膠對泡棉來說簡直是救星！另外，我可以借兩個安全別針嗎？萬一膠水在熱天裡黏不住，我想從內側用別針固定當作備用方案。）
Cosplayer B: Of course! Here is the super glue and a few safety pins. Take your time fixing it, there's no need to rush. We also have some wig tape and wet wipes if you need to clean your hands afterward.
（當然沒問題！這是瞬間膠跟幾個安全別針。慢慢修，不用急。如果你們等一下需要擦手，我們這也有假髮專用膠帶跟濕紙巾。）
Sora: Thank you so much! You are incredibly kind. *[Leo helps apply the glue and pins]* Leo, can you press the strap down firmly and hold it for about thirty seconds? The super glue needs a bit of pressure to bond properly with the foam.
（非常謝謝你！你人真的太好了。*［Leo 幫忙塗膠並別上別針］* Leo，你能用力把帶子壓住並保持大約30秒嗎？瞬間膠需要一點壓力才能跟泡棉完美接合。）
Leo: Got it, holding it down now. One, two, three... okay, it feels very solid now! Let's secure it with the safety pins from the inside just like you planned. Wow, look at that! It's perfectly back in place and you can barely see the repair from the outside.
（收到，正在壓著。一、二、三... 好的，現在感覺非常牢固了！我們按照妳的計畫，從內側用安全別針把它固定住吧。哇，妳看！它完美地歸位了，而且從外面幾乎看不出修補的痕跡。）
Sora: *[Testing the movement]* Wow, it works perfectly! It feels even sturdier than before. *[Returning the tools]* Thank you so much again for saving my day! Can I offer you some snacks as a token of our appreciation? I have some Taiwanese pineapple cakes here.
（*［測試活動度］* 哇，效果完美！感覺比之前還要更結實了。*［歸還工具］* 再次非常謝謝你拯救了我的一天！我可以送你們一些零食來表達我們的一點謝意嗎？我這裡有一些台灣的鳳梨酥。）
Cosplayer B: Oh, pineapple cakes? I love them! Thank you so much! I'm just glad I could help. In the cosplay community, we always have to watch each other's backs. Have an amazing time at the parade!
（噢，鳳梨酥嗎？我超愛的！非常謝謝你們！我也很高興能幫上忙。在 Cos 圈裡，我們本來就應該隨時互相照應。祝你們在遊行玩得非常開心！）
